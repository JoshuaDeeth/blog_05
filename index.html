What is E-Paper Display Technology & How Does It Work?

https://www.ynvisible.com/news-inspiration/what-is-e-paper Februaru 18th 2022

Epaper technology is used to make displays resemble printed paper. Epaper displays do not use much power as they have whats called "image memory". Epaper dont emit light, but reflect it, making them confortable to read even under direct sunlight
